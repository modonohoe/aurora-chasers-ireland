Disclaimer: Please note that while Ireland is located in a prime position for viewing the Northern Lights, they are not always visible due to various weather conditions. We cannot guarantee that you will see the Northern Lights during your visit to Ireland. However, we recommend checking the weather forecast and aurora activity beforehand to increase your chances of catching a glimpse of this stunning natural phenomenon.

Welcome to Aurora Chasers Ireland!

We are two enthusiastic aurora chasers based in Dublin Ireland. The aim of this website is to give you *tips and advice for seeing the Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights) right here in Ireland and provide lots of useful resources. Whether you are living here or just visiting, young or old, a complete novice or an expert - we hope you will find something to help you with your Irish aurora adventure.
