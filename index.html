Important disclaimer: please note that while Ireland is located in a prime position for viewing the Northern Lights, they are not always visible due to various weather conditions. We cannot guarantee that you will see the Northern Lights during your visit to Ireland. However, we recommend checking the weather forecast and aurora activity beforehand to increase your chances of catching a glimpse of this stunning natural phenomenon. May the odds be in your favour!

Who are Aurora Chasers Ireland?

We are a team of enthusiastic aurora chasers based in Dublin Ireland. The aim of this website is to give you *tips and advice for seeing the Aurora Borealis (Northern Lights) right here in Ireland and provide lots of useful resources. Whether you are living here or just visiting, young or old, a complete novice or an expert - we hope you will find something to help you with your Irish aurora adventure. Further to our disclaimer above, the aurora is only visible during certain times of the year - you can learn more about this *here


We have also compiled some stunning photos by incredible photographers for you to enjoy in our Gallery section in the meantime.
